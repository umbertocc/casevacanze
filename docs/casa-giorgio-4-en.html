Key features

3 Bedrooms 1 Bathroom Ground floor Outdoor areas
Casa Giorgio 4 is a welcoming independent ground-floor apartment in a small building with four twin apartments, just 40 metres from the golden sandy beach of Torre Pali. Perfect for families with children and groups up to 6 guests, it offers everything you need for a memorable Salento holiday: 3 bedrooms that can be arranged as doubles, a full kitchen, air conditioning, satellite TV and a practical outdoor shower for when you return from the sea.

The apartment has a private outdoor area at the front that can be used for free parking, plus a second furnished outdoor area at the back with table and garden chairs, ideal for open-air lunches and dinners. Within 50 metres you will find a supermarket, bars, restaurants and all main services. Torre Pali beach is perfect for families thanks to its shallow, safe water.
