⭐ Key features

2 Bedrooms 1 Bathroom 70m� First floor
Casa Bellavista 1 is a first-floor apartment in the town centre, just 100 metres from the sandy beach of Torre Pali and only 3 minutes by car from the famous Maldives of Salento. Set in a quiet area, it includes a large stair hall, kitchen area, two spacious bedrooms with 2 double beds and 1 single bed (up to 5 guests), a panoramic furnished outdoor area with table, chairs, sofa and satellite TV for open-air meals, a bathroom with shower, air conditioning, microwave, washing machine and a free street parking pass.
Ideal for: families and couples looking for a comfortable, central stay close to the sea, with all services within walking distance.

📍 Location advantages

Just 100 metres away you will find the sandy beach of Torre Pali, while bars, restaurants, grocery shops, bakery, ice cream shop, newsagent, tobacco shop, small harbour for excursions and children's play areas are all within walking distance.
