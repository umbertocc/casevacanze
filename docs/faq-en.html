How can I book an apartment?
You can book by contacting us directly on WhatsApp (+39 388 658 70 80), by phone, or through the contact form. We usually reply within 1 hour with availability and booking confirmation. We do not rely on platforms like Booking or Airbnb, which helps us keep rates more competitive.
Is a deposit required?
Yes, we require a 20% deposit of the total amount at the time of booking. The deposit can be paid by credit card or bank transfer. The remaining balance is paid before or on arrival according to the agreed arrangement.
What is the cancellation policy?
- Free cancellation up to 90 days before check-in (full refund of the deposit)

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2 months in advance, especially for July and August when apartments fill up quickly. For last-minute stays, contact us to check immediate availability.

Check-out: by 10:00
For early or late check-in, please contact us in advance. Depending on availability, late arrivals after 22:00 may involve a small extra fee.
How does key handover work?
We welcome you personally on arrival for key handover and a quick tour of the apartment. We explain how appliances and air conditioning work and share recommendations for beaches and restaurants. At check-out, you hand the keys back directly to us.
Are the owners available during the stay?
Yes, we are local and available! We are happy to provide information, tips for enjoying your holiday such as restaurants, hidden beaches and excursions, and help with any issue or need during the stay. Our support is discreet but reliable, so you can enjoy a relaxed holiday.
What should I bring?
The apartments are fully equipped, except for bed linen unless otherwise agreed. We recommend bringing your own beach towel, sunscreen, flip-flops, swimwear and personal toiletries. The kitchen includes crockery, pots and utensils.
